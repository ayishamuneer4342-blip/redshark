### 2. Service Cards (The Interactive Grid)

* **Design:** White cards with a very faint light-grey border.
* **Hover State:** When a user hovers over a card, the border turns **Glow Red**, and a small red arrow icon "slides" in from the left.
* **Animation:** **Staggered Fade-in.** As the user scrolls down, the cards don't just appear; they pop up one by one with a 0.1s delay between them.

### 3. The "Process" Timeline (The Flow)

* **Design:** A thin red line that "draws itself" as the user scrolls down the page.
* **Visual:** Each step (1, 2, 3) is a white circle with a red outline. When the scroll hits that section, the circle fills with a solid red gradient.
* **Animation:** Use **Lottie animations** (subtle moving line art) for each step (e.g., a magnifying glass for "Discovery," a gear for "Execution").

2. **Parallax Imagery:** For the "Team" or "Hero" images, use a slight parallax effect where the image moves slower than the scroll‚Äîit adds instant "depth" to a white background.
